Review

1. Between DOCTYPE html and head, lang is missing. This may cause html checker misindetify the language.
2. In head, meta charset is missing. It is important to specify the character encoding for the HTML document.
3. In ul, the links a shall be put into li. ul always relates to li directly. 4. The links used don't belong to local links or external paths. It may cause the links fail to open.
5. In the img width and height should be put into style="", otherwise, the parameters make no change.
6. In the img, after scr="", there should be a alt="", So if the image failed to be shown, the alt text could replace it.
7. In the form, under Emails:, the name shall be "email" instead of "lastname", otherwise, the content may fail to match the text.
8. In footer, span makes no difference compared with the effect without span. Regarding the defination of span in html, The span tag provides no visual change by itself.
